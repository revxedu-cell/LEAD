LEAD:
OUR NEW SCHOOL

LEAD 359 opened on September 4, 2024, and is the first new school building built in District 7 in more than 10 years.

Located at 639 St. Ann’s Avenue in the Bronx, our state-of-the-art facility brings 547 seats, which include 96 seats for District 75. The building boasts 32 classrooms including 4 pre-k, 3 kindergarten, 15 standard, 2 special education and 8 District 75 classrooms.

Our fully accessible and air-conditioned 4-story school building features:

  • Read and speech resource rooms;
  • A science resource room;
  • Art and music classrooms;
  • A library;
  • Gymnatorium;
  • Medical, guidance and administrative suites;
  • Staff lunch and conference room;
  • Parent and community room;
  • Kitchen and cafeteria room;
  • Exercise room;
  • And, an outdoor play area.
