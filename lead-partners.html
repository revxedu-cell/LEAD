Partner With Us

We’re building learning that connects school to the real world—and we need your help.

Ways to Get Involved

  • Volunteer: Lead a workshop or mentor a scholar.
  • Collaborate: Share a real-world problem your business is working on that scholars could help solve.
  • Donate: Give money, equipment, or materials to support scholar projects and classrooms.
  • Sponsor: Provide bigger or ongoing support for a specific space, program, or event (like a lab, exhibit, or showcase).

Every partnership helps scholars build skills, confidence, and purpose.

Ready to be involved?

Apply your talents and/or resources towards cultivating the next generation of changemakers.

Current and Past Partners

Bronx Children's Museum

The Bronx Children’s Museum needed a way to make changing landforms engaging and understandable for kids. They turned to us, and we delivered a kid-friendly handbook to add to their educational toolkit. If you have a challenge, we’re here to help you solve it too!

Catalyze

Catalyze is a funder who supports our collaboration with RevX work to provide young people with career-connected learning experiences, helping them build confidence and agency to navigate future education and career opportunities.

NY Department of Environmental Conservation

The NY DEC needed to train more environmental educators to support their mission of protecting New York’s natural resources. Partnering with us, they expanded their capacity to educate and inspire the next generation of environmental stewards.

NewSchools

NewSchools is a funder who supports our collaboration with RevX to evolve the design of our school model. They invest in innovative solutions that expand what’s possible in education.

RevX

RevX is our key partner in evolving our school model toward more rigorous, real-world learning. They collaborate with us on implementation, curriculum design, leadership routines, staffing structure, instructional coaching, codification, training, and more.

Success In Motion

Success In Motion partners with us to support health and wellness for school teachers and staff. They design and implement onsite well-being programs, creating a unique approach to workplace wellness in education.

Transcend

Transcend partners with us to provide operational support, helping evolve our school model.
